Meet the Creatures

Lumi

Lumi is a shy, glowing mushroom creature that illuminates the darkest caves. They say Lumi's light can help crops grow faster.

Sparky

Sparky is a mischievous firefly-like companion who loves to play pranks. Despite being a bit of a troublemaker, Sparky is incredibly loyal.

Mossy

Mossy is a gentle giant, a forest spirit covered in soft moss. Mossy is a calm and wise presence, often found napping in sunbeams.
